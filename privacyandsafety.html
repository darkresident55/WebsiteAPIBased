1. Data Collection

In Short: We collect only the minimum required data to operate our services, and we do not share it with third-party developers.

Information We Collect

  • Your email address (if provided during account creation)
  • Your IP address and device ID while logged in
  • Basic usage analytics to improve our services
  • User preferences and settings

We use your IP address to verify your identity, which helps with bot verification and keeps our services safer. This information is only accessible by our internal team and we do not use external data processors for this data.

2. Data Usage

We use the information we collect to provide, improve, and personalize our services.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Communicate with you about updates and support
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse
  • Monitor usage and analyze trends

Data Retention

  • Your IP address is cleared after every logout
  • If your account is banned, your IP and device ID are securely stored in a blacklist to prevent abuse
  • We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy

3. Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information.

Security Measures

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Limited access controls and authentication
  • Regular security monitoring and updates
  • Secure backup and recovery procedures

Compliance

We comply with applicable data protection regulations including:

  • GDPR (European Union, including Germany)
  • APPI (Japan)
  • CCPA (California)
  • Other applicable US privacy standards
